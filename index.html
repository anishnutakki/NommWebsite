Common Questions

How does nomm identify allergens in restaurant dishes? +

nomm uses advanced AI technology to analyze restaurant menus, ingredient lists, and preparation methods. We partner with restaurants to get verified allergen information and continuously update our database with the latest menu changes.

Can I trust the allergen information provided by nomm? +

We work directly with restaurant partners to verify allergen information and cross-contamination protocols. However, we always recommend confirming with restaurant staff for severe allergies, as kitchen practices can vary.

What dietary restrictions and allergens does nomm support? +

nomm supports all major food allergies (nuts, dairy, gluten, shellfish, etc.) and dietary preferences including vegan, vegetarian, keto, paleo, halal, kosher, and many others. You can customize multiple restrictions in your profile.

Will nomm work in my city? +

We're launching in major metropolitan areas first and rapidly expanding to smaller cities. Sign up for our waitlist to be notified when nomm becomes available in your area.

Is nomm free to use? +

nomm offers a free tier with basic restaurant recommendations. Premium features like detailed ingredient analysis, priority support, and advanced filtering options are available with a subscription.
